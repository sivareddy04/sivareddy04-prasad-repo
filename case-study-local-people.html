The Challenge: Seasonal Food Scarcity

Our ground-level monitoring in rural Andhra Pradesh reveals a recurring crisis: seasonal unemployment among rural laborers. When the harvest season ends, thousands of families lose their primary source of income, leading to immediate food scarcity and economic distress.

"కొంత మంది కలిసి మరికొంత మందికి సహాయం చేయడం — This isn't just a slogan; it's a necessity when our neighbors are struggling for their next meal."

Our Field Intervention

Mana Vivekam agents visit these remote clusters to verify reports of distress. We don't just log data; we provide immediate funding for survival essentials. This includes:

  • Monthly Dry Ration Kits
  • Emergency Medical Funding
  • Cooking Fuel Assistance
  • Direct Cash Transfers for Rent
