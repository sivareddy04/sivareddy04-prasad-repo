Empowering Single Women

Building independence through small business grants and vocational support.

Restoring Dignity to Widows & Single Mothers

In many rural communities, widows and unsupported single mothers face not only financial hardship but also social isolation. Without a stable income, they struggle to provide education and nutrition for their children. Our mission is to transform these women from beneficiaries into business owners.

"Economic independence is the most powerful tool for social change. When a woman earns, her entire family rises."

The Micro-Welfare Strategy

We provide customized support based on the skills and circumstances of the individual. Our current focus areas include:

  • Industrial Sewing Machines
  • Small Business Startup Grants
  • Tailoring & Embroidery Training
  • Financial Literacy Workshops
