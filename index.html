Flowchart of Assistance

Here is exactly how information moves from an orphan or single mother to secure website funding allocation.

1. Orphan Child Identified

A child, widow, or vulnerable community entity is located experiencing critical basic necessity deficits.

2. Local Gatherer Audit

Verified local field partners visit, validate live status parameters, log geolocation, and take photos.

3. Local Organization

Mana Vivekam central structure processes structural needs lists and approves listing on portal.

4. Your Direct Donation

Donors complete secure payment pipeline online. Audit trails update automatically to build complete trust.
